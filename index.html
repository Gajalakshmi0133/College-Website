About the College

M.O.P. Vaishnav College for Women (Autonomous), established in 1992, is a premier institution affiliated with the University of Madras. Located in the heart of Chennai at Nungambakkam, the college has been a beacon of women's education, offering a blend of academic excellence and holistic development. With a commitment to empowering women, the college provides a dynamic learning environment supported by state-of-the-art infrastructure and a dedicated faculty.

Infrastructure & Facilities

Library

The college boasts a well-equipped library with a vast collection of books, journals, and digital resources to support academic and research activities.

Laboratories

State-of-the-art laboratories, including computer labs, audio-visual labs, and specialized labs for various departments, facilitate practical learning.

Auditorium & Seminar Halls

Modern auditoriums and seminar halls equipped with advanced audio-visual systems host various academic and cultural events.

Cafeteria

A hygienic and spacious cafeteria offers a variety of nutritious food options catering to diverse tastes.

Sports Facilities

Comprehensive sports infrastructure includes facilities for indoor and outdoor games, promoting physical well-being among students.

Wi-Fi Enabled Campus

The entire campus is Wi-Fi enabled, ensuring seamless internet connectivity for academic and research purposes.

Campus Life

M.O.P. Vaishnav College offers a vibrant campus life enriched with various clubs, societies, and events. Students actively participate in cultural fests, seminars, workshops, and community service programs, fostering holistic development and leadership skills.
